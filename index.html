Touko Aozaki

Born in August 8 to a middle class family, she has one younger sister. Always taking an interest in the arts, Aozaki Touko studied abroad for two years in England at the age of 14 before returning to Japan. After her high school years, she once again traveled to London for education in the Clock Tower academy.

By the age of 20, Touko became one of the top artists specializing in doll crafting. In addition to doll crafting, Touko takes and interest in digital graphics. She is also well known for her architectural constructions. One of the recent projects that she had created blueprints for was the Ogawa spiral apartment complex
