Who is Kawhi Leonard?

  • Kawhi Leonard is a professional basketball player for the San Antonio Spurs.
  • He's been the best defensive player in the league 2 years in a row.
  • In 2014 he led his team to an NBA championship and was named the NBA Finals Most Valuable Player

Random Facts

  • Kawhi was named after the Hawaiian island of Kauai.
  • He signed a $94 million contract last year, but still drives his 1997 Chevy Tahoe and uses coupons to eat at his favorite restaurant WingStop
